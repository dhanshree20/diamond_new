Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What defines the Diamond design style?
Our style is a sophisticated blend of modern minimalism and timeless classic luxury. We focus on high-quality materials, neutral palettes with bold accents, and functional elegance.
Do you work internationally?
Yes. While our main atelier is in Beverly Hills, we have successfully completed residential and commercial projects in London, Dubai, Paris, and Tokyo.
What is the typical timeline for a project?
Timelines vary based on scope. A single room furnishing may take 6-8 weeks, while a full structural renovation often spans 6-12 months from concept to completion.
