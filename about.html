Who We Are

At Diamond Home Decor, luxury isn’t just a product—it’s an experience. Based in Pune, we are known for offering premium furnishing solutions that transform everyday interiors into extraordinary lifestyle spaces.

With years of experience, an eye for elegance, and a strong network of artisans and global suppliers, we provide everything your space needs—from custom upholstery to statement decor, all tailored to refined tastes.
